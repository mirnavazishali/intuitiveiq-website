The Enterprise Trust Layer

Built to survive your CISO, your auditor, and your board.

Explainable — every answer ships with its why.

Understand why a recommendation, decision, or action was produced — reasoning, sources, and signal weights attached. "Why did the AI say that?" is answered in one click, not a forensic investigation.

Traceable — and your data never leaves your platform.

Track which data, model, agent, and policy contributed to every outcome. Your data stays where it lives — no extra copies to secure. Works with the compliance controls already in your platform (HIPAA, GDPR, and more).

Auditable — mistakes get caught, not shipped.

A judge agent verifies every AI answer against the underlying data; disagreements are held back and flagged. The full evidence chain — which source, which rule, which transformation, which agent — exports in one click when the regulator calls.

Accountable — your experts stay in control.

Ownership is assigned across human approvals, automated actions, and policy-driven decisions. High-confidence mappings auto-promote; borderline ones route to your stewards. Nothing reclassifies silently. No black-box decisions.
