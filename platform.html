The platform

Four pillars. One Decision Intelligence Engine.

The engine connects everything your business knows — your definitions, rules, policies, and data — so AI understands your business the way your best people do, acts on that understanding safely, and shows its evidence for every decision. The four pillars below are what make it run.

⌬

Knowledge Fabric

Create trusted business understanding from fragmented enterprise data. Without it, AI sees data. With IntuitiveIQ, AI understands business context.

  • One shared definition for every business term
  • Your rules, policies & relationships — captured once, used everywhere
  • AI answers grounded in your company's own knowledge
  • Context that keeps up as your business changes
  • Builds on the systems you already run

Agentic Orchestration

Turn intelligence into governed action. Without orchestration, agents remain experiments. With IntuitiveIQ, agents become governed digital workers — agents must explain themselves, or they don’t deploy.

  • AI agents that carry work end to end — not just chat
  • Every action runs under your policies
  • People approve the moments that matter
  • Every agent in one registry — no shadow agents
  • Guardrails block disallowed actions before they run
  • Scale from one workflow to hundreds without losing control

One operating model for many models, many agents, many tools, and many business workflows — consistent controls across cloud, data, and application ecosystems.

  • Use the best AI model for each job — without new risk
  • One set of rules across every model, agent & tool
  • Usage, cost & performance visible in one place

Inside Agentic Orchestration · the agent control plane

Ambition without sprawl. Control without the brakes.

Your AI leaders want agents that are smarter and work together. Your infrastructure leaders want every agent accounted for and under control. IntuitiveIQ delivers both at once — the agent control plane behind Agentic Orchestration, where every agent is known, governed, and accountable before it ever touches your business.

For the VP of AI · Chief Data Officer

Agents that are smarter, and work as a team.

Ship reliable agent systems faster. Multiple agents coordinate on real work and hand off to each other, draw on your governed business knowledge so every decision is grounded in trusted context — not guesswork — and are measured for quality and reliability before they go live and watched after. The result is more capable AI you can put into production with confidence, and more value from every model you run.

For the CTO · CISO · VP of Infrastructure

No shadow agents. No blind spots.

Every agent lives in one registry — nothing runs outside your view. Role-based access decides who can invoke which agent, guardrails block disallowed actions before they happen, and every action and access lands in an audit trail you can stand behind. The sprawl of ungoverned agents becomes a single, controlled fleet you actually run — the control you need to say yes to scale.

Agent registry

Every agent catalogued and versioned in one place — what it is, what it can do, and what it’s allowed to touch. The end of agents you didn’t know existed.

Role-based access

Permissions, enforced. Only authorized people and systems can invoke a given agent or model — checked on every request, not assumed.

Guardrails on every action

A code of conduct on every step an agent takes. Policy is checked on each action, and disallowed moves are blocked before they run — not flagged after the damage.

Multi-agent orchestration

Agents collaborate across a governed workflow — handing off to each other and reasoning over your trusted knowledge — so real work gets carried end to end, not just chatted about.

Audit & monitoring

A complete, reviewable record of what every agent did and who accessed what, with live oversight of every action — so you can see how an outcome was reached and prove it later.

Continuous evaluation

Performance reviews for agents. Quality and reliability are measured before deploy and on an ongoing basis — so only agents that earn trust stay in production.

It’s all one engine: the Knowledge Fabric gives agents trusted meaning, and this control plane turns that meaning into governed action — so the Decision Intelligence Engine doesn’t just decide, it acts, explainably and under your control.

The Enterprise Trust Layer

Built to survive your CISO, your auditor, and your board.

Explainable — every answer ships with its why.

Understand why a recommendation, decision, or action was produced — the reasoning and the sources it relied on attached. "Why did the AI say that?" is answered in one click, not a forensic investigation.

Traceable — and your data never leaves your platform.

Track which data, model, agent, and policy contributed to every outcome. Your data stays where it lives — no extra copies to secure. Works with the compliance controls already in your platform (HIPAA, GDPR, and more).

Auditable — mistakes get caught, not shipped.

A judge agent verifies every AI answer against the underlying data; disagreements are held back and flagged. The full evidence chain — which source, which rule, which model, which agent — exports in one click when the regulator calls.

Accountable — your experts stay in control.

Ownership is assigned across human approvals, automated actions, and policy-driven decisions. What the system is confident about goes ahead automatically; anything borderline is routed to your experts to confirm. Nothing reclassifies silently. No black-box decisions.
